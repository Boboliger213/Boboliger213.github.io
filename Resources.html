Take the Next Step Forward

Explore a variety of resources to enhance your high school experience and prepare for the future.

Resources

Volunteering

  • [Volunteer Canada] - Discover and sign up for volunteer opportunities across Canada.
  • [Ontario Public Libaries] - Volunteer at your local library! They have programs for teens and your local library will have a page exactly like this one from toronto.

General Information

  • [University Information Ontario] - Access a wide array of information on Ontario Universities.
  • [Ontario University Applications] - Apply to your favoured University programs. Make sure to hand in applications before the set deadlines.
  • [Grade 12 Reddit Community] - A community of grade 12 students in Ontario. Feel free to ask questions and take influence from their experiences.

Competitions & Hackathons

  • [Waterloo Resources] - Find various competitions on mathematics and computer science.
  • [Devpost] - Find online or in-person Hackathons to apply for. Beginner-friendly options are available!
  • [Major League Hacking] The largest and most diverse hackathon events in the world.

Scholarships

  • [Youth of Canada] - Apply to a large collection of scholarships for Canadian youth.
  • [RBC Scholarships] - Discover the Royal Bank of Canada's valuable scholarships.
  • [Student Life Network] - Access resources and scholarship opportunities aimed at Canadian students.

Academic Resources

  • [Waterloo Courseware] - Free courses on computer science and mathematics by University of Waterloo
  • [Jensenmath] - One of the best online resources for learning mathematics.
  • [Harvard's CS50x] - Sign up for a free harvard online course on computer science and earn a certificate.

Extracurriculars and Internships

  • [Hack Club] - Join a community full of teen hackers and access free resources for high school students.
  • [Shad Canada] - Apply to live on university campus for a month, aimed at grades 11 and 12.
